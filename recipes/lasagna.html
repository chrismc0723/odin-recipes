A simple Lasagne Recipe

This is the end result of a simple yet delicious lasagna recipe that can be made for 2 to 4 people

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500g of mince
  • Red Leicester Cheese
  • Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tomato Puree
  • Garlic Bread
  • smooth tomato bolognese sauce
  • Lasagne creamy white sauce

Steps

  1. PLace the pan onto the hob and Heat the hob to point 4 on your cooker
  2. PLace the 500g of mince into the pan and cook until browned all over
  3. Season with some salt and pepper
  4. put some tomato puree into the mix, about the length of your pinky "FYI I am not a cook lol"
  5. Finally pour in the full bottle of smooth tomato bolognese sauce
  6. now your mince is ready we can start creating the lasagne
  7. Place a layer of lasagne sheets into your chosen dish
  8. then put a layer of mince
  9. then put a layer of white sauce
  10. repeat until you are near the top of your dish
  11. when you are near the top, have the last set be lasagne sheet then white sauce
  12. then cover the top in a mix of the 2 cheese's, be sure to grate them
  13. have your oven set to 200 degrees celcius and put the dish into the oven
  14. after 10 minutes place the garlic bread into the oven aswell
