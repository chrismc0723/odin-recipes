A tasty Macaroni Cheese

This is the result of a quick and easy mac and cheese recipe that everyone can enjoy

Ingredients

  • Garlic Bread
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 350g of pasta, your choice what kind
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 500g of cheese, can be 500g of 1 cheese or split it between different cheese's

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200C
  2. Boil the pasta until it's nearly cooked then drain, feel free to add a pinch of salt before boiling the pasta
  3. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a pan. add milk gradually along with the flour while whisking until you have a lump-free sauce
  4. Simmer for 5 minutesm whisking all the time until thickened then mix in half of your grated cheese
  5. Once that's done, pour the cheese sauce into the pasta
  6. fill your chosen dishes with the pasta and cheese sauce mix and top with the rest of your grated cheese
  7. Stick your dishes in the oven until you have a nice crispy brown ish cheese top
  8. put the garlic bread in the oven at the same time as the macaroni cheese
  9. 10 to 20 minutes later they should be ready to enjoy
